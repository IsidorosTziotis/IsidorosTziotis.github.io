Active Projects

Federated Learning with Incentives.
The focus of this project lies on the incentives of the agents participating in a Federated Learning framework and the formation of stable clusters with small aggregated loss.

Improving on Adaptive Gradient Methods.
In this work we aim to simplify the analysis of AdaGrad and remove the bounded gradient assumption maintaining the rates of the latest literature.

Cubic Regularized Newton's Method in Decentralized Systems.
In this project we focus on the problem of convergence to first and second order stationary points in multi-agent systems utilizing second order information.
