Active Projects


Federated Learning with Incentives.
In this project we study the incentives of participating agents in the Federated Learning framework and the formation of stable coalitions with small aggregated loss.

Improving on Adaptive Gradient Methods.
In this work we aim to bridge the gap between stochastic gradient descent and adaptive methods in both convex and non-convex regimes.

Cubic Regularized Newton's Method in Decentralized Systems.
In this project we focus on the problem of convergence to first and second order stationary points in multi-agent systems utilizing second order information.
